Client Information

Client's Name: Levana Benabe. Owner/Final decision maker.
Client's Business Name/Website: A Home Away From Home Daycare / homeawayfromhome.com
Brand New Website or Redesign? Brand New
Type of Website: Marketing, Identity
Client Goals: Online presence to advertise her new in-home daycare service. She would like to display rates, description of service/care, hours, and a gallery of pictures.

Audience, Issues and Approach (AIA)

Audiences Issues, Concerns, Needs Approach

Parents of Infants/First time seeking childcare

Most likely in their 20s or 30s and employed, could be either gender, from any income/education/job. Likely to have internet access either at work or at home (or both) and decent knowledge of computers.
  • Cost
  • Location/Hours
  • Safety
  • Naps, feedings, diaper changings
  • Will the newborns be separated from older children? Is the house "babyproofed"?
  • Display rates that competitive within the current childcare market.
  • Display hours and location, along with an interactive map.
  • Include a page for newborn services that includes things like how often the babies will be held, fed, changed, put down for a nap. Include ratios of adults to children/newborns.
  • Include pictures of the newborn area/room.

Parents of Toddlers/Preschoolers

Most likely in their late 20s or 30s and employed, could be either gender, from any income/education/job. Likely to have internet access either at home or at work and decent knowledge of computers.
  • Cost
  • Location/Hours
  • Safety
  • Daily activities - Do they offer daily outside playtime? How are the children prepped for preschool/kindergarten?
  • Discipline - How is unwanted behavior corrected?
  • How does the provider help children develop socially and emotionally?
  • Nutrition - what do meals consist of and how often are they provided?
  • Display rates that competitive within the current childcare market.
  • Display hours and location, along with an interactive map.
  • Describe the childcare providers philosophy and typical daily routine/schedule, including outside time and pre-k curriculum.
  • Provide a link to the current or an example of the meal schedule.

Parents of School Aged Children

Most likely in their 30s or early 40s and employed, could be either gender, from any income/education/job. Likely to have internet access either at home or at work and decent knowledge of computers.
  • Cost. Are there discounts for younger siblings?
  • Location/Hours
  • Safety
  • Daily activities - is there a curriculum established? Do they offer help with homework?
  • Discipline - How is unwanted behavior corrected?
  • Is there transportation to/from the child's school?
  • Are snacks or meals provided before or after school?
  • Display rates that competitive within the current childcare market. Include discounts for siblings (if any) and discounted rate for school children since they are only there part time.
  • Display hours and location, along with an interactive map.
  • Include the care provider's philosophy regarding child care on the home page.
  • Describe any differences to the daily schedule/meals as compared to what the younger children will adhere to.
