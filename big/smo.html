Social Media Optimization (SMO)

So what is “Social Media Optimization” anyway?

According to Wikipedia, “Social media optimization (SMO) is the use of a variety of outlets and communities to generate publicity and increase the awareness of products, service brands or events. Types of social media involved include RSS feeds, social news and bookmarking sites, as well as social networking sites, such as Facebook, Twitter, video sharing websites and blogging sites”¹. It is similar to Search Engine Optimization (SEO) in that the goal is to increase traffic to your website.

Why is it so important?

Social media has changed the way we live. More people are using the internet and social media than ever before. Social media sites have streamlined the ability to share information, products, and services. According to Maryam Mohsin of the Oberlo blog, “there are 3.5 billion social media users worldwide, and this number is only growing. That equates to about 45% of the current population”. As of 2018, “Roughly two-thirds of U.S. adults (68%) now report that they are Facebook users”, with over 2.32 billion active users worldwide (and that number is just Facebook users). To continue with some statistics from Maryam’s blog, “an average of 3 hours per day is spent on social networks per person” and “73% of marketers say that social media marketing has been somewhat effective or very effective for their business”². With stats like this, it’s easy to see how utilizing social media platforms can increase your business.

How can I make the most of SMO?

There are multiple ways that you can optimize the use of social media. Taking careful consideration of your target audience while choosing a platform will have the greatest impact of optimization. For instance, utilizing groups within Facebook to promote services for a particular area will yield greater results versus choosing to promote via blogs. Choosing a visual centric platform such as Instagram to showcase products will have greater impact than choosing a non visual platform. Or if you are a business to business or career counselor, LinkedIn may be the route to take. Another very important way to use social media is to ensure that you have a social media share tool bar in your site, “reliable statistics have shown that this increases virality by up to an incredible 700%”³, according to SEO Zones.
