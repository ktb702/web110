Welcome!

Within this website you will find several pages on research topics such as Accessibility, Social Media Optimization (SMO), and HTML Forms. Also included are some google tools, such as a map and calendar. And don't forget to check out the lightbox gallery!

To interview for a web job, thoughtfully contribute to a web team, and provide guidance for web clients, it's vital that we keep learning to stay on top of current and coming web topics and coding practices. Several ways developers can grow and maintain their web knowledge and skills are:

  • Read magazines, blogs, reddit, books, or listen to podcasts.
  • Go to events, conferences, or meetups.
  • Participate in open source communities.
  • Take additional courses.
